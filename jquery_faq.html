State Parks of Texas

Big Bend

  • Located near Terlingua, Texas
  • Covers 300,000 acres
  • Opened to the public in 2007
  • Has 5,500 foot paved airstrip

Davis Mountains

  • Located near Fort Davis, Texas
  • Built by the Civilian Conversation Corps
  • Opened in the 1930s
  • Has miles of trails

Enchanted Rock

  • Located near Fredericksburg, TX
  • Covers 1640 acres
  • Opened in 1978
  • Has 250,000 visitors per year
